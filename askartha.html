🤔 Why Machine — How markets connect
Why do US Fed decisions move Indian stocks?
🏦
US Fed raises interest rates
US bonds become more attractive — safer, higher yield
💸
FIIs sell Indian stocks
They move money to US bonds where returns are now better
📉
Large FII selling puts pressure on Nifty
Nifty falls even if Indian economy is fine — foreign money leaving
💻
Weaker rupee helps IT companies
TCS, Infosys earn in USD — same dollar = more rupees = higher profit
📌 One Fed decision in Washington creates: US bond yield → FII flows → Nifty movement → Rupee → IT profits. This is why watching the Fed matters even if you only invest in India.
Why does crude oil price affect your portfolio?
🛢️
Crude oil rises globally
India imports 85% of crude — import bill rises by crores every day
💱
India's trade deficit widens
More rupees leave India — rupee weakens against dollar
✈️
Aviation & paints hit hardest
Jet fuel is IndiGo's biggest cost. Asian Paints margins compress directly.
📌 Oil rising: buy IT/Pharma (exporters benefit from weak rupee). Avoid Aviation/OMCs (importers suffer). This is sector rotation 101.
